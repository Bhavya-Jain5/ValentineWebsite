Kiss Day

for the late night calls
You know that moment right before you fall asleep on call — when your words turn into hmms?
Those little hmms say more than anyone's ever said to me.
I don't need you to stay awake. I just need you to stay.
One day there won't be a phone between us. Just us. Every night.
Until then — goodnight, puku baby.
This one's for every kiss the screen stole from us. ♡
